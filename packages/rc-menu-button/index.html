Keyboard Controls

  • Enter, Space, or ArrowDown on trigger: Open menu, focus first item
  • ArrowUp on trigger: Open menu, focus last item
  • Escape: Close menu, return focus to trigger
  • ArrowUp/ArrowDown in menu: Navigate items
  • Home/End in menu: Jump to first/last item
  • Enter/Space on item: Activate and close menu
  • Click outside: Close menu
